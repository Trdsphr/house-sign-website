Privacy Policy

SignCraft 3D

SignCraft 3D respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

SignCraft 3D allows users to design personalised house signs. We may collect information that you provide voluntarily, such as:

  • Your name (if provided during ordering or contact)
  • Email address for communication
  • Custom sign designs created within the app
  • Order details if purchasing a product

We do not collect unnecessary personal information.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected may be used to:

  • Create and process your personalised sign orders
  • Communicate with you about your order
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve the SignCraft 3D experience

Design Data

Your custom sign designs created inside SignCraft 3D may be stored to allow you to continue your design process and complete orders.

We do not sell or share your designs with third parties for advertising purposes.

Payments

Payments, if available, are processed securely through trusted payment providers or Apple's payment systems. We do not store your complete payment card information.

Third-Party Services

SignCraft 3D may use third-party services that help provide app functionality, analytics, hosting, or payment processing. These services may process limited information according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

SignCraft 3D is not designed to collect personal information from children under the age required by applicable laws.

Your Rights

You may contact us to request information about your personal data, request corrections, or ask for deletion where applicable.

Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
