Refund Policy

SignCraft 3D

At SignCraft 3D, we create personalised handmade house signs based on customer designs. Because each product is customised specifically for each customer, our refund policy is designed around personalised products.

Custom Products

Personalised house signs cannot usually be returned or refunded if they have been made according to the approved design provided by the customer.

Before production begins, customers should carefully check all details including:

  • Name spelling
  • House numbers
  • Design choices
  • Material selection
  • Colours and finishes

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If your sign arrives damaged, defective, or different from the approved design, please contact us as soon as possible.

We may request photographs or information about the issue to help us investigate and provide an appropriate solution.

Requesting a Refund

To request assistance with an order, please contact us with:

  • Your name
  • Order details
  • Description of the issue
  • Photos if the product is damaged

We will review each request and respond as soon as possible.

Cancellation

Orders may only be cancelled before production has started. Once a personalised sign has entered production, cancellation may not be possible.

Delivery Issues

We aim to deliver orders safely and within the estimated delivery timeframe. Delays caused by shipping providers or circumstances outside our control may occur.
